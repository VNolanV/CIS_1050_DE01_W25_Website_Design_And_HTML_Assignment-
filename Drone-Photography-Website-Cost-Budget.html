Considering Your Cost/Budget

When considering a photography drone, one of the first aspects to evaluate is your budget. Understanding how much you're willing to spend can help narrow down your options and prevent you from overspending on features you may not need. Cost encompasses more than just the initial price of the drone; it also includes any additional expenses like accessories, batteries, and software.
