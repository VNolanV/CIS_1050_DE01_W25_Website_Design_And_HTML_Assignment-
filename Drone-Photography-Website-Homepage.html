Introduction

It is said that the best camera in existence is the human eye. While that is true, memories are often fleeting and can be forgotten. Thus, attempts have been made to capture moments in life in a more permanent way. These attempts led to the concept and development of the modern-day camera used today. However, with the evolution of technology and its affordability/accessibility, cameras have been taken to new heights in the form of photography drones.

Today, photography drones have become widely accessible and affordable to the point where anyone can buy and use them. However, there are many different factors you should consider before hopping on the market to buy your first drone. This website and its articles will go over everything that you need to know before purchasing your first drone.
